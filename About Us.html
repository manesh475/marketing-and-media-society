MARKETING & MEDIA SOCIETY

Purpose Of Society

  • To promote SZABIST through different online-offline platforms.
  • To provide a platform for students to share their ideas.
  • Design promotional material (Electronics & print).
  • To share various events and activities to different stake holders.
  • To arrange various activities and events with collaboration to marketing students faculty.
